This Privacy Policy explains how Xtend-AI, LLC ("Xtend-AI," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and the My AI Bartender mobile application (collectively, the "Services").

1) Who we are

Company: Xtend-AI, LLC
Product: My AI Bartender
Contact email: support@xtend-ai.com

2) What we collect

A. Information you provide

Depending on how you use the Services, you may provide:

  • Account and login info (for example, email address or social sign-in): Authentication is handled by our identity provider. We do not store your password.
  • Age verification info: You will be asked for information to confirm you are of legal drinking age (for example, date of birth).
  • Profile info: Such as display name or a system-generated alias (if enabled).
  • User content: Such as cocktail notes, custom recipes, favorites, bar inventory, and messages you choose to send through social sharing features (if enabled).

B. Photos and camera data (Smart Scanner)

If you use Smart Scanner, you provide a photo (or camera capture) for ingredient/bottle identification.

  • The photo is processed to provide the feature.
  • We do not use your photos for advertising.
  • Unless explicitly stated in the app at the time of capture, we do not retain bar photos after processing.

C. Voice and audio data (Voice AI)

If you use Voice AI, your device captures audio and connects directly to our Voice AI provider using WebRTC. Our backend issues an ephemeral session token that allows this connection.

During a voice session:

  • Your audio is processed to generate responses.
  • Speech-to-text transcription may be generated (for example, to show you what you said and what the AI said).
  • We store voice usage and session metadata such as session identifiers, duration (seconds used), and token counts to enforce quotas, prevent abuse, and operate the feature.

Transcripts: Depending on product settings and the version of the feature, transcripts may be:

  • shown only in-app during the session and not stored long-term, or
  • stored as conversation history associated with your account.

If transcripts are stored, they are used to provide and improve the Voice experience for you (for example, showing conversation history and debugging issues).

D. Usage, diagnostics, and device information

We collect typical technical data needed to run and secure the Services:

  • App interactions and feature usage (for example, quota usage, session duration)
  • Log and diagnostics data (crash reports, performance metrics)
  • Device and network information (device type, OS version, approximate location derived from IP, and similar signals)
  • Identifiers used for security and fraud prevention

E. Subscription and payment information

Purchases are processed by Apple App Store and/or Google Play (and/or our subscription management provider). We generally receive:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive), product tier, renewal dates
  • Transaction identifiers and receipt validation data

We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

  • Provide support and respond to requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
